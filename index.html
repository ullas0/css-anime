Bounties on the Straw Hat crew

Monkey D. Luffy

Monkey D. Luffy was the founder of the Straw Hat Pirates. Influenced by his idol Shanks, Luffy always dreamt of becoming a pirate and starting his own crew ever since he was a kid. Luffy set out to become the next pirate king and told Shanks that he would put together a greater crew than his. That was the beautiful start of the Straw Hat pirates, and Luffy set sail on his own.

Roronoa Zoro

Zoro was the first person to join Luffy’s crew as the Swordsman and became the second official member of the Straw Hat Pirates. He was working as a bounty hunter and had earned the fearsome title of “Pirate Hunter Zoro” before meeting Luffy.

Vinsmoke Sanji

Sanji was the fourth member to join the Straw Hat pirates as the Cook at the end of the Baratie arc in One Piece. One of the important things for Luffy was food, and he had luckily recruited the best chef in the world for his crew. Sanji dreamed of finding All Blue, a sea legend that refers to the only place where the North Blue, South Blue, East Blue, and West Blue seas meet in the One Piece world. This was also the dream of Sanji’s mentor, Zeff.

Jimbe

Jimbe was the ninth and latest member to join the Straw Hat pirates as their Helmsman. Among everyone else, Jimbe was the only one we waited years to join the crew. Luffy befriended Jimbe in the Impel Down arc and invited him during the events of the Fishman Island arc. As Jimbe was an ally of the Big Mom Pirates, he had to cut ties with her before and promised Luffy to join the crew soon.

Nico Robin

Nico Robin was the sixth member to become part of the Straw Hat Pirates in One Piece. She joined the crew as their archaeologist. Nico was first seen as an antagonist during the Arabasta arc but later joined the crew to pursue her dream of uncovering the history of the world, especially during the Void Century.

Nami

Nami was the second member to join the Straw Hats as the Navigator during the Orange Town arc. But she tricked Luffy and joined the crew only to steal money from them to reclaim her village from the Arlong pirates. She was a member of the Arlong pirates during that time. But after Luffy learned about this incident, he rescued her village by beating down the Arlong pirates.

Brook

Brook was the eighth member to join the Straw Hat Pirates as the crew’s Musician in the One Piece anime. He joined the crew at the end of the Thriller Bark arc as Luffy always wanted to have a musician in his crew.

Franky

The crew needed a Shipwright at the end of the Post-Enies Lobby arc, fortunately, Franky became the seventh member. Similar to Robin, he was initially considered to be a villain of the Straw Hats, but he later turned into an ally and made up for his mistakes by building the Thousand Sunny, an absolutely beautiful ship for the Straw Hats. He dreamed of building a ship and traveling all over the world

Tony Tony Chopper

The fifth member to join the Straw Hat Pirates crew was the Doctor Tony Tony Chopper. Every crew needed to have a doctor on their ship as anything could happen during the perilous journey in the oceans. Chopper wasn’t a human, but a reindeer. He got this human hybrid form after eating the Hito Hito no Mi devil fruits.

Usopp

Usopp was the third member to join the Straw Hat Pirates as the Sniper. He initially led the Usopp Pirates in his village, but he joined Luffy’s crew after the events of the Syrup Village arc. After Luffy befriended him and helped save his friend, Kaya, Ussop accepted the invitation to the Straw Hats to become a brave warrior of the sea like his father.
